Artic Fox

The fox's thick coat keeps its body warm at 104 degrees Fahrenheit.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes Lagopus
  • Average Length: 18-27 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 3 to 6 years
  • Habitat: Artic and alpine tundra

Artic foxes adapt to frigid cold temperatures, thanks to their furry paws, short ears, and short muzzles. They can grow up to 3 feet long and weigh 6-17 pounds. As the seasons change, the color of their coat can change to white, brown, or blueish gray.
